INSTRUCTIONS

Their are 108 cards as follows:
19 Blue cards - 0 to 9
19 Green cards - 0 to 9
19 Red cards - 0 to 9
19 Yellow cards - 0 to 9
8 Skip cards - 2 each in Blue, Green, Red and Yellow
8 Reverse cards - 2 each in Blue, Green, Red and Yellow
8 Draw 2 cards - 2 each in Blue, Green, Red and Yellow
4 Wild cards
4 Wild Draw 4 cards

RULES:
If you are playing scoring enabled then the first player to play all of the cards in their hand in each round scores points for the cards their opponents are left holding. The first player to score selected score wins the game. And if you are playing scoring disabled then the first player to play all the cards WINS.
Scoring of the player wins calculated as follows:
All number cards (0-9):  Face Value
Draw 2:   20 Points
Reverse:  20 Points
Skip:   20 Points
Wild:   50 Points
Wild Draw 4:  50 Points

HOW TO PLAY:
First shuffle the deck and distribute it among players. Then pick 1 card from the shuffled deck and place it over to start the play. The player who wins the toss have the first turn and he will start to play. The player is only allowed to play a card which matches the color or number on the play card. If it is a special type than he can also play a same special card of different color. Special Cards such as: Draw two: Add 2 cards from the remaining deck to opponent. Skip: Skips the turn of the opponent. Reverse: Return the turn again to the player. Wild: This card can be played any time and player can choose your color. Wild Draw Four: This card can also be played any time and player can choose color and also add 4 cards to opponent.
